# Project Synopsis: Fitness App

## Project Title
**RetroFit**: "Unlock Your Fitness Potential"

## Team Members
- Nihaal Virgincar
- Pratik Nayak
- Rajivkumar Naik

## Introduction
In an era where fitness routines can often feel monotonous or overwhelming, RetroFit offers a refreshing and engaging approach to staying active and healthy. We use the charm of the past with the power of modern technology to transform your fitness journey.

## Target Users
People who want to improve health, diabetes patients, etc.

## Objectives
1. Helps plan exercise routines that are tailored to the unique goals and needs of the user.
2. Keeps a record of user data.
3. Provides insights into the progress which motivates the user.
4. Helps with nutrition and diet plans.
5. Notifies the user regarding their routine.

## Features
1. **User Authentication**
   - Registration and login.
2. **User Details**
   - Record of fitness data.
3. **Exercise Routine Planner**
   - Plan and manage the exercise routines and patterns.
4. **Progress Data**
   - Displays progress statistics.
5. **Meal Preparation**
   - Suggests premade healthy meal recipes.
